Essential Questions

• How do living and nonliving things work together?
• How do humans affect ecosystems?

Biotic vs. Abiotic

Biotic (Living) / Biótico
Plants 🌱
Animals 🐾
Bacteria
Abiotic (Nonliving) / Abiótico
Water 💧
Sunlight ☀️
Temperature 🌡️

Adaptations

Physical Adaptation / Adaptación Física
Body part that helps survival (fur, camouflage).
Behavioral Adaptation / Adaptación Conductual
Action that helps survival (migration, hibernation).
Sentence Frame:
The ______ helps the organism survive because ______.

Compare Ecosystems

Land: soil, trees, temperature changes
Freshwater: low salt water, rivers, lakes
Marine: salt water, oceans, coral reefs

Vocabulary

Abiotic – nonliving / no vivo
Biotic – living / vivo
Climate – long-term weather / clima
Precipitation – rain or snow / precipitación
Marine – ocean ecosystem / marino
Freshwater – low salt water / agua dulce
Desert – dry land / desierto
Tundra – very cold land / tundra
